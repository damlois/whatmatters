Inspiration

A verse and a nudge to live in the moment.

Which of you by taking thought can add one cubit unto his stature?
— Matthew 6:27 (KJV)

Worry doesn't add a single hour—or cubit—to your life. So take a breath.

Love is above all.

Everything else - people, friends, nature, wellness—finds its place when we lead with love. So put love first. The rest follows.

Love never fails.

— 1 Corinthians 13:8

Live in the moment

Remind yourself to enjoy what you already have. Breathe. Look up. Put the phone down sometimes.

Gain knowledge. Help others. Love a little more. It doesn't have to be loud—it just has to be real.
